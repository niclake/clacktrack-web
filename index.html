ClackTrack

Catalog your mechanical keyboard collection

Coming to iPhone and iPad Fall 2023

Keep up to date with ClackTrack updates

By signing up, you agree to receive emails from me in regards to ClackTrack beta and release announcements.

Keep track of your collection

Track your keyboards, switches, keycaps, and accessories, as either individual components or assembled boards.

Chart your collection history

See trend lines of your collection size over time, including completed boards, your Hurley Number, or even (gasp) how much you've spent on this hobby.

Show it off

Install widgets to show your Hurley Number, photos of your collection, and more. Easily share your builds to Reddit, Twitter, and other social media sites.

Sync across your devices

Whether you have your iPhone or iPad to hand, ClackTrack will sync your changes seamlessly via iCloud. Keep track of all your builds, photos, and more, wherever you are!

Privacy Policy

ClackTrack will not collect any data about you or your device. Synced data will use private Apple-provided services that I have no access to.
